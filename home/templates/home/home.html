Hi, I'm Louise. I started Because Care Matters Training & Consultancy (BCM) to help health & social care professionals feel supported and prepared to deliver a high quality of care. Throughout my career in care I have experienced many trials and tribulations, where I have not always felt as supported as I would have liked. 

Why choose BCM?

I offer high quality Health & Social Care Training and Consultancy for all your Social Care needs. I can provide training ranging from an induction program meeting CQC requirements to designing a bespoke tailor made course to your needs, policy and procedures.

I have 25 years’ experience in the Health and Social care industry where I started as a care worker and progressed to a registered manager then business manager. I also worked as a regional trainer, progressing to a recruitment and training manager.

I am based centrally and I can travel throughout the UK. I can work the hours to suit your business, weekends and evenings included. I am fully committed to ensuring we stamp out bad practice within the social care industry by providing up to date, engaging and relevant training.
