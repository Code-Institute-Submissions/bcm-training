Because Care Matters Training (BCM) was started to help health & social care professionals feel supported and prepared to deliver a high quality of care. Throughout my career in care I have experienced many trials and tribulations, where I have not always felt as supported as I would have liked. 

I offer high quality Health & Social Care Training, and Consultancy for all your Social Care needs. I can provide training ranging from a generic induction program to designing a bespoke tailored made course to your needs, policy and procedures.

I have 25 years’ experience in the Health and Social care industry, where I started as a care worker and progressed to a registered manager then business manager, I also worked as a regional trainer, progressing to a recruitment and training manager.

I have worked with a diverse range of service users throughout my career including; Elderly Care, Mental Health, Dementia care. My background has enabled me to develop my knowledge and skills for different settings. 

I am based centrally and I can travel throughout the UK. I can work the hours to suit your business, weekends and evenings included.I am fully committed to ensuring we stamp out bad practice within the social care industry, by providing up to date, engaging and relevant training.
